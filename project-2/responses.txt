Briefly describe one way how data travels on the internet.

Data travels on the internet by computers pulling files from other computers and displaying the information on those files to end-users.

What is the relationship between an IP address and the DNS?

The Domain Name System keeps track of all of the names with their Internet Protocol address.

What is a cookie?

Cookies are information stored in the browser that are accessed by websites to obtain basic information, such as favorite color, location, that sort of thing.
